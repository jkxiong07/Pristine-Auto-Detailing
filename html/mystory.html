Pristine Auto Detailing

My Story

My brothers and I have always been into cars. We grew up playing Gran Turismo and Need For Speed on the Play Station 2 and our passion for cars grew as we became adults. Jesse, my older brother was the first of us to learn how to detail cars working for a variety of auto detailing shops in the metro area of Minneapolis, Minnesota. In 2018, he began his own detailing side gig in his own garage to make an extra source of income. But Jesse wanted to do something bigger. In 2020, Jesse opened Humble Crew Detailing, his first auto detailing business in Plymouth, Minnesota.

Shortly after I returned from my missionary service for my church, I began working for Jesse and learned how to detail cars as well. I learned how to professionally clean cars, perform paint corrections, apply ceramic coatings, as well as interior detailing. The first cars I detailed were my younger brother's BMW E46 M3 and a red Chevrolet C5 Corvette. Now, as a student studying Information Systems at Brigham Young University, I want to use my skills to generate a source of income while I pursue my degree.
